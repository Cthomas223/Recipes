Fresh Tomato Soup

Description

Fresh, amazing, and easy to make tomato soup.

    Ingredients
  • 4 cups chopped fresh tomatos
  • 1 sliced onion
  • 4 whole cloves of garlic
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons white suger
    Directions
  1. In a pot over medium heat, combine the tomatos, onion, cloves and chicken broth
  2. Bring to boil, and gently boil for 20 minutes to blend all of the flavors
  3. Remove from heat and run the mixture thruogh a food mill into a large bowl,
    then discard anything left over in the food mill
  4. In the empty pot, melt the butter over medium heat. Stir in the flour to make a roux.
  5. Cooking until the roux is medium brown, gradually whisk in a bit the tomato mixture so that no lumps form.
  6. Season with suger and salt and adjust to taste.
