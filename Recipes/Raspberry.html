Raspberry Blackberry smoothie

Description

A simple yet delicious smoothie!!

    Ingredients
  • 1 small banana
  • 1/2 cup blackberries
  • 1 cup fresh raspberries
  • 1 (6 ounce) contianer vanilla yogurt
  • ice cubes
    Steps
  1. Place all ingredients into a blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. Enjoy
